About Megan Moroney

The original "emo-cowgirl" was born in 1997 and grew up near Savannah Georgia. Megan started making music when she moved to Nashville in 2020 after graduating from the University of Georgia. Originally, she was an Accounting major at UGA but quickly realized that this wasn't she wanted to do and switched her major to something music related. After her big move to Nashville, she released her first song "Wonder" in 2021. Her career didn't really take of until she released her song "Tennessee Orange" in 2022. After the release of that song, Moroney started to grow a much larger following. Keep scrolling to learn about more of her songs!
